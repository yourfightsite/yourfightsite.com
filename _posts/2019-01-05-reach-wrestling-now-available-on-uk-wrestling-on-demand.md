We at Your Fight Site are pleased to announce that [REACH Wrestling][1] has
joined our flagship wrestling video on demand network, [UK Wrestling On Demand][2].

REACH Wrestling becomes the fourth channel to be part the UK Wrestling On Demand
network, joining [Pro Wrestling Chaos][3], [Shropshire Wrestling Alliance][4],
and [Ultimate Pro Wrestling][5].

The price of the UK Wrestling On Demand plan will remain the same. So for just
**£3.99 a month** you can access **over 200 hours of video** on demand content
from four wrestling promotions!

To subscribe, head to [ukwrestlingondemand.com][2]
