--- excerpt: "We’ll soon be introducing Your Fight Site SSO, allowing you to use all of Your Fight Site’s services with one set of credentials." layout: post title: "Your Fight Site SSO coming soon" ---

In late 2014 we launched Your Fight Site VOD, a pro wrestling video on demand website. We’re pleased with the number of registrations on the website, and Your Fight Site VOD is the first product in a line we’re launching, with more to come.

Most sites require registration to use, and the ones we have in the pipeline are no different. However, we at Your Fight Site hate having to remember different log ins for different sites. That’s why we’ll soon be introducing Your Fight Site SSO.

Your Fight Site SSO will allow you to use the same log in credentials across all of our sites. You’ll also be able to use your social network accounts (such as Facebook and Twitter) to log into Your Fight Site applications.

We’ll be implementing Your Fight Site SSO on Your Fight Site VOD first. You won’t need to do any thing, and your email address and password you use to log in with will remain the same. It does mean you will be able to use any new Your Fight Site services straight away with the same email address and password, though!

If you have any questions about Your Fight Site SSO, then email us at info@yourfightsite.com.
